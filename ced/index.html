cyber education day 2020-2021
This fall, CRDF Global will implement two online Capture the Flag (CTF) based hackathons for cybersecurity students and practitioners in Moldova and six Balkan countries, including Bosnia, Montenegro, Albania, Kosovo, Macedonia and Serbia. These regional events, set to take place in the Balkan region in October and Moldova in November will bring together participants in a collaborative setting to identify cybersecurity vulnerabilities, solve problems, and advance secure technologies. The theme of these hackathons will focus on the Internet of Things (IoT), the system of interrelated computing devices, or "smart" objects, with the ability to receive and transfer data without human interaction.
"Collaboration on this type of knowledge is very important to create new systems of protection involving our Ukrainian students with programs like this at Purdue helps them see what cybersecurity looks like on the business side."
Mykhailo Verych, CRDF Global Cybersecurity Deputy Program Manager, Ukraine Office
"A cybersecurity expert is not an expert just in one part of cybersecurity. We need to know every part just to have a full map in our brains to do our job correctly. This experience has helped me expand my knowledge and capabilities in cybersecurity. For that, I am ever grateful. Thank you, CRDF Global, for this opportunity!"
Nataliia Tusina, 2019 hackathon winner
